## The High-Stakes Launch Day

Launching a SaaS is more than just "Turning it on." It's a coordinated effort of technology, marketing, and security. In 2026, the bar for a "Professional" launch is higher than ever. Use this checklist to ensure you are ready for the spotlight.

## 1. Technical Infrastructure

- [ ] **SSL/TLS**: Valid certificates on all domains.
- [ ] **Sitemap & robots.txt**: Generated and submitted to Search Console.
- [ ] **Global CDN**: Assets served from Edge nodes near your users.
- [ ] **Auto-Scaling**: Ready to handle a 10x spike in traffic during the launch.

## 2. Security and Compliance

- [ ] **GDPR/CCPA**: Privacy policy live and consent banners working.
- [ ] **OWASP Check**: No obvious injection or auth vulnerabilities.
- [ ] **Audit Logs**: Recording all critical admin actions.
- [ ] **Secrets**: All keys moved out of the code and into a secret manager.

## 3. Operations and Support

- [ ] **Monitoring**: Sentry and OpenTelemetry alerting is active.
- [ ] **Support Channel**: Intercom, Crisp, or a simple email ready for user feedback.
- [ ] **Knowledge Base**: Basic documentation for your core features.
- [ ] **Transactional Emails**: Welcome and Password Reset emails are verified.

## 4. Billing and Commerce

- [ ] **Stripe Production Mode**: Live keys swapped and webhooks verified.
- [ ] **Taxes**: Stripe Tax configured for EU/US sales.
- [ ] **Success Metrics**: Tracking MRR, Churn, and LTV from Day 1.

## 5. Performance and UX

- [ ] **Lighthouse Score**: 90+ across Mobile and Desktop.
- [ ] **Empty States**: Every dashboard looks great for a new user.
- [ ] **Analytics**: PostHog or Heap tracking user journeys.

## Conclusion

A successful launch is the result of thousands of small, right decisions. By following this checklist, you eliminate the "Looming Disaster" factor, allowing you to focus on celebrating your milestone and talking to your first customers.
